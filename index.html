Physical Activity

Physical activity is a major combatant against the epidemic of obesity. Whether it is active recreation pursuits like athletic leagues, fitness and dance programs, trails and active amenities like playgrounds and pools, our parks provide natural locations for organized physical activities, as well as unstructured spaces that overtly or subtlety encourage physical activity.

Lessing of Anxiety

Visiting parks tends to lower anxiety in mentally stressed people. It stands out as one of the best place to get back into the right state of mind.

Providing Connection Space

With the internet’s prevalence, it’s become more common to go from work to home without changing up the routine. Parks give individuals a space for meeting their neighbors in person.

Child Development

When kids get to play outside instead of indoors, it increases the positive impact of physical activity. Kids who spend time outside have better test scores and better cognitive functions
